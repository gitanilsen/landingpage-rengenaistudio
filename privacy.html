Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 1, 2025

RenGen AI Studio ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and website (collectively, "the Service").

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address and authentication credentials via Firebase Authentication.
  • Uploaded Images: Product photos you upload for AI processing. These are sent to Google's Gemini API for image generation and are not permanently stored on our servers.
  • Payment Information: Payment transactions are processed securely by Razorpay. We do not store your credit card numbers, UPI IDs, or banking details on our servers.
  • Usage Data: We collect anonymous usage statistics such as credits consumed and feature usage to improve our service.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Service.
  • To process your payments and manage your credit balance.
  • To send verification emails and important service notifications.
  • To improve our AI generation quality and app performance.

3. Image Processing & Storage

Images you upload are sent to Google's Gemini API for AI processing. These images are processed in real-time and are not permanently stored on our servers or Google's servers after processing is complete. Generated images exist only in your browser/app session until you download them.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): For authentication, database, and cloud functions.
  • Google Gemini API: For AI image generation and processing.
  • Razorpay: For secure payment processing.

Each of these services has their own privacy policy, and we encourage you to review them.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted communications (HTTPS/TLS), secure API key management through Google Cloud Secret Manager, and Firebase Authentication for access control.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal data.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Opt out of non-essential communications.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at rengenaistudio@gmail.com.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at rengenaistudio@gmail.com.
